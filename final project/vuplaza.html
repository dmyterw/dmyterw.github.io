Whats Here?

Buildings

Wilson Library

A brick building that is both a library and campus services building. Zoe's Bookside Bagels is located inside.

Performing Arts Center (PAC)

A brick building that houses the following departments: College of Fine and Performing Arts, Music, and Theatre and Dance.

Viking Union

A large brick, concrete, and glass building that houses many student services. The student services include: Associated Students, Bookstore, Ethnic Student Center, LGBTA, KUGS 89.3 FM, Off-Campus Housing, and the Outdoor Center.

Sculptures

For Handel

A large structural sculpture made from red painted steel, created by Mark di Suvero in 1975.

Middle Fork Bend

A video installation comprised of four screens, created by Claude Zervas in 2015.

Bay View Station

A series of wooden platforms and metal walkways, created by George Trakas in 1987.
